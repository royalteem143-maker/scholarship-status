Exam Instructions (Important)

  • The event lasts 7 days with one quiz per day (10–15 MCQs).
  • Each quiz has a fixed duration. Complete within time.
  • Do not use unfair means. Any malpractice will lead to disqualification.
  • Use a single device per student. Do not share accounts.
  • Results will be compiled after Day 7 and announced within 3–5 days.

How to participate on quiz day

  1. Open the exam link sent on WhatsApp.
  2. Login with the registered mobile number.
  3. Attempt the quiz within the given time.
  4. Submit answers and check daily leaderboard.
